===================================
Lightsaber Example
===================================

Game
---------------------------------
* Simple demo. Press space bar to activate / deactivate the lightsabers
* Move lightsabers to visualize trail

Pre-Condition
---------------------------------
* Unity 2019.3.3f1+

Installed Packages:
* PostProcessing 2.3.0

Used Assets
---------------------------------
* Pocket RPG Weapon Trails
https://www.assetstore.unity3d.com/en/#!/content/2458

* Audio
Various free sources on the Internet
